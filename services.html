About Services

At Shredex, we’re passionate about skiing and snowboarding. We are dedicated to providing top-notch services tailored to each individual’s needs.

Our services range from personalized coaching for beginners and advanced riders, to organizing thrilling mountain tours and family-friendly skiing and snowboarding experiences. Each service is designed with a focus on safety, skill improvement, and most importantly, enjoyment. Explore our services to find the perfect snowboarding adventure for you!
