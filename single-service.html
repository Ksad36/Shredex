Single Service

At Shredex, we understand that every skier and snowboarder is unique. That’s why we offer our Single Service - Personalized Coaching. This service is designed to cater to your individual needs, whether you’re a beginner hitting the slopes for the first time or an experienced rider looking to refine your skills. Our certified coaches will work with you one-on-one, providing personalized guidance based on your skill level, goals, and style. They’ll help you master everything from basic techniques to advanced tricks, ensuring you progress at your own pace.

"The mountains are calling and I must go." John Muir

But it’s not just about technique. Our coaches also focus on safety, ensuring you know how to protect yourself and others on the slopes. Plus, they’ll teach you about snowboard maintenance, so your gear is always in top shape. With Shredex’s Personalized Snowboarding Coaching, you’ll gain confidence, improve your skills, and most importantly, have a great time on the slopes. So why wait? Book your session today and start your snowboarding adventure with Shredex!

Common questions

What does the Single Service include?

Our Single Service includes personalized coaching tailored to your skill level and goals. It covers everything from basic techniques to advanced tricks, safety measures, and snowboard maintenance.

I’m a beginner. Is the Single Service suitable for me?

Absolutely! Our Single Service is designed to cater to snowboarders of all skill levels. Our coaches will provide guidance based on your current abilities and help you progress at your own pace.

How long does a Single Service session last?

The duration of a Single Service session can vary depending on the specific needs and goals of the client. We recommend contacting us directly for more detailed information.

Can I book a Single Service session for a group?

Our Single Service is typically one-on-one coaching. However, we do provide a seperate group coaching upon request. Please contact us for more details.

What safety measures are in place during the Single Service sessions?

Safety is our top priority. Our coaches are trained in safety protocols and emergency procedures. We also ensure that all participants are equipped with the necessary safety gear.

How do I book a Single Service session?

You can book a Single Service session through our website. If you have any issues or need assistance, feel free to contact our customer service.
